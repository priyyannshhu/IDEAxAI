## 🤖 How It Works

1. **Describe Your Idea** — Enter a brief description (e.g. *"AI fitness coaching app"*)
2. **AI Analyzes & Plans** — Powered by Google Gemini, the AI researches market trends, competitors, and crafts a tailored blueprint
3. **Get Your Blueprint** — Receive a complete plan covering startup overview, problem statement, target audience, MVP features, tech stack, revenue model, marketing strategy, and development roadmap
